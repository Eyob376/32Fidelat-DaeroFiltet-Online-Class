Login & Account Help

  • How do I log in as a Member?
    Use the email you registered with and your password (studentLastName + “daero”).
  • How do I log in as Admin?
    Admin credentials are provided by the school.
  • I forgot my password.
    Contact support to reset your password.
  • I didn’t receive my login email.
    Check your spam/junk folder or contact support.
