DAEROFILTETT LANGUAGE & CULTURE CLASS

Our Mission is Fostering Identity & Pride

The aim of Daero Filitet is to empower our children to foster a sense of identity, pride, belonging, and to strengthen communal bonds. We encourage all Eritrean families to join the program and register their children.


By connecting with their language and culture, children gain a deeper understanding of who they are and where they come from, building a strong foundation for their future.


The Tigrinya Language (ትግርኛ)

A Semitic language spoken in Eritrea and the Tigray Region of Northern Ethiopia, rich in history and cultural significance.


Linguistic Roots & Development

Tigrinya is descended from Ge'ez, the classical and liturgical language of the region. It began developing as a spoken language around the 7th up to 9th century. It shares roots with other Eritrean languages like Tigre and Arabic. It uses the Ge'ez script (Fidel) and has a rich grammar system, with verb conjugations that change based on person, number, and gender.
